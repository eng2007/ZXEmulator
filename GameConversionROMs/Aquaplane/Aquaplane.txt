Aquaplane
Quicksilva Ltd, 1983
48K Spectrum
Converted to 16K ROM cartridge by Jimmy Smith, 2020

Modified the border timing so it works on 128 machines and even 60Hz machines (this is the bit that proved interesting because 
the scrolling routines were too slow). Also added Fuller joystick routines and the joystick can now be used whilst entering a hi-score.
