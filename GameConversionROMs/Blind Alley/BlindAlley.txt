Blind Alley
Sunshine Books Ltd, 1983
16K Spectrum
Converted to 16K ROM cartridge by Jimmy Smith, 2020

This game was changed slightly for the Timex TS2068, in that it supported Timex joysticks and had a different hi-score routine and attract screens. These have been imported into the original ZX release. Also added other joystick options and changed the speed of text printing.
